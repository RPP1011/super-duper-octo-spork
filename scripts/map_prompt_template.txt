Top-down fantasy dungeon map for an adventurer guild branch mission.

Theme:
- Dark-fantasy city district at night.
- Main objective is to disrupt a summoning ritual in a cathedral plaza.

Layout:
- 3 clear lanes from guild outpost to ritual plaza:
  1) Market Street
  2) Canal Bridge
  3) Shrine Alley
- Include choke points and one mini-boss room before final plaza.
- Mark safe fallback zone near guild outpost.

Readability:
- Simple silhouettes and readable lane boundaries.
- Distinct color coding for friendly route, enemy route, and objective route.
- Keep clutter low for autobattler tactical readability.

Style:
- Stylized 3D concept art, game map overview, high contrast lighting, no text labels.
