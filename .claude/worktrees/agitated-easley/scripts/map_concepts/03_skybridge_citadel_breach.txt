Top-down 3D fantasy mission map concept for a guild-led dungeon assault.

Concept: Skybridge Citadel Breach
- Cliffside fortress district with suspended bridges and rune towers.
- Mission objective is to interrupt a storm-summoning ritual at summit keep.

Required beats:
- Three tactical lanes:
  1) Gate Ramp (frontline attrition lane)
  2) Wind Bridge (risky fast lane with exposure zones)
  3) Tower Steps (defensive lane with staggered choke points)
- One optional side room for resource pickup/recovery.
- Penultimate antechamber encounter before boss platform.
- Final circular summit ritual arena.

Art direction:
- Dawn storm lighting, dramatic clouds, magical wind effects.
- Stylized 3D map blockout with clean tactical readability.
- Distinct lane identity via architecture and color accents.
- No UI, no text labels.
