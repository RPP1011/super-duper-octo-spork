Top-down 3D fantasy dungeon map concept for an adventurer guild mission.

Concept: Sunken Crypt Canals
- Flooded undercity crypt beneath the guild district.
- Objective is to disrupt a necromantic anchor ritual.
- Multi-lane path with water channels and raised stone walkways.

Required beats:
- Three route choices:
  1) Broken Cloister (balanced path)
  2) Floodgate Run (fast but high enemy density)
  3) Reliquary Corridor (safer, slower progression)
- Midpoint miniboss chamber with skeletal war machine.
- Final ritual nave with circular rune platform.
- Distinct choke points and fallback pockets.

Art direction:
- Moody torchlight, wet stone, green necromantic glow accents.
- Stylized readable geometry suitable for tactical autobattler.
- Clear lane boundaries and objective focal point.
- No text labels, no UI overlay.
