Top-down 3D fantasy mission map concept for an adventurer guild branch operation.

Concept: Guild Quarter Siege
- Nighttime district under cult assault.
- Three combat lanes from guild outpost to cathedral ritual plaza.
- Lanes:
  1) Lantern Market (wide lane, many stalls, medium cover)
  2) Canal Bridge (narrow choke lane, heavy defensive value)
  3) Shrine Alley (twisting flank lane with side pockets)

Required beats:
- Safe command fallback zone at guild outpost.
- One elite checkpoint in each lane.
- Final boss space at ritual circle in cathedral plaza.
- Visual signals for tactical readability and autobattler lane clarity.

Art direction:
- Stylized high-fantasy 3D, readable silhouettes, strong lane contrast.
- Blue/amber for allied route cues, red/magenta for enemy pressure.
- No UI, no text labels, no characters posing for camera.
